What are Service Layer benefits?

Decouples complexity
Reuse from multiple entry points
Encapsulation of concern logic
Intuitively know where business logic resides

When is a Service Layer worth it?

Evolving requirements
Growing scope of your org
Expanding or distributed team
Multiple Clients
Building a product

What are Service Layer guidelines?

Must be caller agnostic
Must follow bulkification and limits guidelines
Must be maintained to avoid 'Leaks'
Should follow strict naming conventions
Should handle Unt of Work for transactions
May be synchronous or asynchronous

Global State

Set data-state="something" on a slide and "something" will be added as a class to the document element when the slide is open. This lets you apply broader style changes, like switching the page background.

State Events

Additionally custom events can be triggered on a per slide basis by binding to the data-state name.
